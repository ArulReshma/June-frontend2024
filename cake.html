Blueberry cakecake

This indulgent summer bake is loaded with blueberries and features cream cheese in both the batter and the icing. It’s a delightful combination of flavors! The recipe includes ingredients like sugar, butter, cream cheese, eggs, sour cream, vanilla extract, and fresh blueberries. You’ll find the detailed instructions here.

Black forestcake

The Black Forest Cake is a traditional German dessert that is a must for many special occasions, from a wedding anniversary to a birthday celebration. Black Forest Gateau is traditionally made with layers of chocolate sponge cake soaked in cherry syrup or kirsch with whipped cream and cherries

Fruit cakecake

This moist and dark fruitcake is loaded with yummy-tasting dried fruits. It skips the icky candied peel and citron, focusing on delicious ingredients like dried pineapple, raisins, apricots, dates, and crystallized ginger. You can customize the dried fruits to your liking.
